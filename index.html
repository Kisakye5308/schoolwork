ABOUT

check our team

At coffee time, we're passionate about one thing: coffee. But not just any coffee, we're obsessed with crafting the perfect cup that ignites your senses and leaves you with an unforgettable experience. Our journey began in 2015, with a dream to create a coffee haven where people could escape the daily hustle and savor moments of pure indulgence. From our humble beginnings as a small local coffee shop, we've grown into a vibrant community hub that cherishes the art of coffee-making and the joy of bringing people together. What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality. From sourcing the finest ethically-sourced beans to employing skilled baristas who treat coffee-making as an art form, every step of the process is infused with love and dedication. Our coffee is roasted in-house, ensuring that every sip carries the rich, bold flavors that awaken your senses and keep you coming back for more. But it's not just about the coffee; it's about the experience. Our cozy and welcoming atmosphere invites you to unwind, connect with friends, or simply take a moment for yourself. We believe that a cup of coffee can be the catalyst for meaningful conversations and lasting memories, and we're honored to be a part of your daily routine. Beyond our love for coffee, we are committed to sustainability and giving back to the community that has embraced us. We partner with local farmers and support initiatives that promote environmental consciousness. With each cup you enjoy at coffee you're contributing to a brighter, greener future. We are more than just a coffee shop; we are a family united by our passion for exceptional coffee and genuine human connections. Our team is dedicated to making your experience delightful, and we take pride in the smiles we bring to our customers' faces. Whether you're a coffee aficionado seeking the perfect pour-over or a newcomer curious about the world of coffee, we invite you to join us on this journey of taste, culture, and togetherness. Explore our menu, meet our team, and become a part of the coffee time family. Thank you for choosing coffee time where every cup tells a story.
